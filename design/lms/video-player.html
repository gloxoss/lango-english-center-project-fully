Present Simple Tense

schedule Duration: 12m 34s

The Present Simple tense is the foundational building block of English communication. It is used to express facts, habits, and general truths. In this lesson, we break down the structure for positive, negative, and interrogative sentences.

Key Takeaways

  • Use the base form of the verb for I, You, We, and They.

  • Add -s or -es to the verb for He, She, and It.

  • Auxiliary verbs "do" and "does" are used for negatives and questions.
